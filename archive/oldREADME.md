# BashicuNum.js

An initial attempt at making a base-10 BMS number library. 

## Idea:

```
1 (0) 
2 (0)(0)
3 (0)(0)(0)
10 (0)(1)
20 (0)(1)(0)(1)
100 (0)(1)(1)
1000 (0)(1)(1)(1)
10^10 (0)(1)(2)
10^10^10 (0)(1)(2)(3)
10^^10 (0,0)(1,1)
```
which is agonizingly slow but we are praying that lim(BMS)  is strong enough to beat other large-number libraries
